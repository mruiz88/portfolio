My journey so far...

I’m a former graphic designer from Barcelona, where I spent years working on creative projects and developing a strong sense of visual design. Over time, I became more and more interested in how people interact with technology, which led me to shift my focus and dive into the world of UX/UI design.

With my background in graphic design and the hands-on experience I’ve gained in responsive web design, I’ve been able to bring together aesthetics and usability to create user-centered digital experiences. I’ve always believed that design is not just about making things look great but about making them work well for the people using them.

Technology has been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember. I’ve always been fascinated by the way humans and computers connect, and I’ve always kept up with the latest trends and innovations. Video games have also been a passion of mine, which has given me a unique perspective on interactive design and user engagement.

With a solid background in both graphic design and user-centric digital design, I’ve worked on projects that challenge me to find creative, functional solutions that truly meet user needs. I’m always excited to take on new challenges and continue pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in design.
